About Velvet Bites

A passionate bakery bringing traditional craftsmanship to modern Hyderabad

Established with a passion for exceptional baking, Velvet Bites has become Hyderabad's premier destination for artisanal baked goods. Our master bakers combine time-honored techniques with innovative flavors to create memorable experiences for every customer.

Located in the heart of Hyderabad, we pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. From our signature sourdough to elaborate wedding cakes, every product reflects our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.
